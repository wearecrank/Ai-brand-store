## Target Audience

**Audience Summary:**
ShipFit targets product teams and businesses looking to validate ideas quickly and efficiently before full development

### Product Manager
**Needs & Pain Points:** Validate product ideas quickly, reduce time to market, minimize development waste

**Motivations:** Launch successful products, make data-driven decisions, prove concept viability

### Startup Founder
**Needs & Pain Points:** Test multiple ideas rapidly, conserve resources, identify winning concepts early

**Motivations:** Build successful business, attract investment, achieve product-market fit

### Engineering Leader
**Needs & Pain Points:** Avoid building features that don't resonate with users, focus development on validated ideas

**Motivations:** Improve team efficiency, reduce rework, deliver impactful features
