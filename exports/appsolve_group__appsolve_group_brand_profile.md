## Organisation Info

**Brand Name:** Appsolve Group

APPSolve Group

**Tagline:** Oracle E-Business Suite & Fusion Cloud Specialists

**Industry:** Oracle Enterprise Software Services

**Overview:**
APPSolve Group are UK-based Oracle specialists delivering Oracle E-Business Suite and Oracle Fusion Cloud services globally. They fix security vulnerabilities, modernise infrastructure, and deliver lasting value from Oracle investments. The company has over 100 Oracle professionals including functional consultants, developers, project managers, solution architects, and business intelligence specialists. Core services include NCSC Advisory and Active Security Remediation, Health Check assessments, Cloud Migration to Oracle Cloud Infrastructure, Managed Support, and Reporting & Data solutions. They also provide Oracle Fusion Cloud migration guidance and implementation support. With 12+ years of Oracle E-Business Suite experience and a typical security patch turnaround of 2-4 weeks, APPSolve focuses exclusively on Oracle services across sectors including pharmaceuticals, mining, telecoms, retail, renewable energy, higher education, and gaming and leisure.
