# Organisation Profile: Tofauti

## Organisation Info

**Brand Name:** Tofauti

**Tagline:** We're here to build resilience not reliance among Africa's communities and wildlife

**Industry:** Charity/Non-profit

**Overview:**
Tofauti is an African wildlife and communities charity founded by Crista Cullen MBE. The organisation was created to bring together Crista's two worlds: her elite sporting background (Olympic Gold winner in hockey) and her deep understanding of the challenges facing Africa's animal and human populations, stemming from growing up in Kenya. Tofauti works to build resilience among Africa's communities and wildlife through various projects and initiatives. The team is small but growing, diverse in backgrounds and experiences, united in their goal to make a difference for Africa's wildlife and communities. The charity focuses on understanding the environment and local tribal traditions, working collaboratively with communities to help them adapt and thrive in today's world.

## Purpose & Values

**Brand Purpose:**
For change in Africa to be long-term it needs to be owned by the local people

**Core Values:**
- **We go further together**: The challenges facing African wildlife and communities can only be tackled through an approach that unites, focusing on building teams to create and sustain solutions
- **We equip and empower**: Every Tofauti project is designed to bring local stakeholders along on a journey, giving locals the tools and training to make change happen and sustain it themselves for generations to come
- **We connect the dots**: We understand that everything is connected and apply both depth of knowledge and breadth of perspective to all that we do for African wildlife and communities
- **Change through knowledge**: Through a knowledge-sharing culture, we shift embedded attitudes that are barriers to change, helping local people appreciate their local habitat, wildlife and their relationship to each other
- **Local commitment**: Every Tofauti project is built on side-by-side commitment where locals work just as hard as we do, sharing in the tough bits and taking pride in the results of our combined efforts
- **Long-term measurement**: All partnerships are based on a commitment to long-term project monitoring and evaluation, believing in sustained, deep-rooted change rather than short-lived moments

**Value Proposition:**
We tackle the challenges facing African wildlife and communities through an approach that unites local people with our teams to create and sustain solutions

## Social Proof

**Key Stats:**
- 293 adults and children — now with a sustainable food source
- 2.35 square kilometres — given back to wildlife to roam free
- 9.13 kilometres of fences installed — enabled 8 different types of crops grown

**Testimonials:**
> "So every Tofauti project is designed to bring the communities and local stakeholders on the journey with us."
> — Crista Cullen, Tofauti Foundation (Community-led sustainable solutions)

**Case Studies:**
**Reteti Elephant Sanctuary - Namunyak Wildlife Conservancy** (Wildlife Conservation)
Problem: Orphaned elephants needing sustenance to build strength before returning to the wild
Result: Provided whey protein partnership to assist orphan elephants' recovery

**Rhino Ark** (Wildlife Conservation)
Problem: Human population growth threatening Africa's mountain forest ecosystems and wildlife safety
Result: Electric fencing installed protecting water catchment areas, enabling wildlife safety and crop raid prevention

**Galana School** (Education & Conservation)
Problem: Lack of access to education in rural Kenya denying children opportunities for better futures
Result: Nursery, primary and secondary school built with equal access for girls and boys, embedding conservation curriculum
